History of Google timeline

Here's a time line of Google

  • 1998 - Google was officially founded
  • 2000 - The Google Toolbar is launched along with the Toolbar PageRank (TBPR)
  • 2004 - Google announces Gmail. Local search and personalisation became major trends.
  • 2005 - Google launches the Google Maps. Was and still is a useful tool to help people get around and find directions. Research partnership with NASA
  • 2006 - Google purchase online video sensation YouTube.
  • 2008 - Google launched Google Chrome, a new open source browser and stated that the browser was intended to creat "a better web experience for users around the world". Also in 2008 releases Android OS
  • 2015 - Announces restructuring as Alphabet Inc. Also Google changed their logo
  • 2017 - Google introduces intelligent search - it is a designed to inform and assist, rather than simply giving its users a list of links. It incorporates AI into all of its products, including search, Gmail, AdWords, Google Assistant, and more.
  • 2022 - Google complete 24 years
